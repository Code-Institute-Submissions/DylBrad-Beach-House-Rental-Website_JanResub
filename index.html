The Property

Luxury privately enclosed house, located 100 meters from the sea. Cool off in our refreshing natural pool, grab a cocktail from our bar and enjoy all this propety has to offer.

Upon entering the property, you will enter the social area. Here you can access our lounge with a staffed cocktail bar and restaurant. There is also an entertainment area with many activities, as well as a natural salt water pool.

The rooms are entered through sliding glass double doors. Each room has a private lounge area, equipped with a smart TV, sofa and mini bar. High speed Wifi connection is available throughout the property.
